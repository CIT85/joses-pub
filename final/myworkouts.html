Exercise Benefits
Barbell Squat This exercise increases muscle mas, enhanced power, faster sprints and better mobility.
Smith Machine Lunges This exercise offeres greater stability and balance compared to free weight lunges. Some of the benefits are increased muscle isolation, which can focus on the glutes and quads. Imporved glute activation, can be effective for targeting the gluteus maximus.
Leg Extension This exercise benefits quadriceps strengthening, knee joint stability, and safe for beginners.
Bulgarian Split Squat Some of the benefit of the Bulgarian split squat, it builds lower body strenght and muscle growth, particularly the glutes, hamstring, quadriceps.
